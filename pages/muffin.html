Charamel muffin

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 100 g sugar
  • 100 ml vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 200 ml milk
  • 50 g flaked almonds
  • 100 ml salted butter caramel
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the eggs with sugar, salt and vanilla extract. Whisk carefully.
  2. Add the vegetable oil.
  3. Mix the flour with baking powder and add with the milk to the mixture.
  4. Prepare the muffin moulds with baking paper to avoid sticking.
  5. Fill half of your muffin moulds with the batter.
  6. Place a teaspoon of caramel and fill the rest of your mould.
  7. Decorate your muffins according to your taste.
  8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in a preheated oven at around 360 degrees F.
  9. Pour some melted caramel on top of the muffins when they ready and warm.
